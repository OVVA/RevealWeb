How Reveal Works

A unique approach to exploration that transforms ordinary journeys into extraordinary adventures.

Step 1

Begin With a Shrouded Map

When you first start with Reveal, your entire world map is covered in a mysterious fog. Unlike traditional maps that show everything at once, Reveal invites you to physically explore your surroundings to unveil what lies beneath.

  • Initial 5km radius around your home location is revealed
  • Areas you've never visited remain shrouded in fog
  • Special landmarks peek through the fog as tantalizing destinations

Initial Fog Map Visualization

Hover to reveal image

Step 2

Explore to Reveal

As you move through the world, Reveal tracks your path and automatically lifts the fog around areas you've visited. This creates a personalized map that reflects your unique exploration journey.

  • GPS tracking works in background to preserve battery life
  • Intelligent algorithms optimize tracking accuracy
  • View your exploration percentage for different regions

Exploration Path Visualization

Step 3

Unlock Achievements & Comparisons

Every exploration milestone earns you achievements that celebrate your journey. Reveal also compares your routes with historical explorers, showing you where your path intersects with legendary journeys.

  • Earn badges for discovering new areas and landmarks
  • Compare your path with historical routes like the Silk Road
  • Build your explorer profile with achievements and statistics
