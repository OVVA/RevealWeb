Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main purpose of the Reveal app?

The Reveal app is designed to track users' locations, allowing them to document their travels and compare their explorations with historical explorers and past empires.

How can I stay updated on the app's launch?

You can easily subscribe for updates by providing your name and email address on our landing page. We'll notify you as soon as the app is ready!

Can I sync the app with my smartwatch or other devices?

This feature is not currently available.

On which platforms will the app be available, and what is the release timeline?

The app will initially launch on Android and as a web version, followed by an iOS release.

Does the app track or store my information on servers?

No, the app does not collect or store your travel information or track you in real-time unless you specifically enable this feature.

I have a great idea for a feature! How can I share it?

We welcome your suggestions! You can submit your feature ideas through the form on our landing page. We'll review them and may even include them in our development roadmap.
